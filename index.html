How much does it cost?

Our standard package starts at $3,000/month.

Can I just hire freelancers to do this?

You could—but you'd need to find, hire, manage, and train a thumbnail designer, a motion designer, a copywriter, a long-form editor, a short-form editor, and a content strategist.

Then you'd need to build a system that gets all of them aligned and delivering on time every week.

We take all of that off your plate—with one team, one process, and one monthly rate.

How many episodes do you edit each month?

We edit up to four episodes per month as part of the standard package. If you're producing more than that, we can customize a plan to match your publishing schedule.

What's included in the base package?

Per episode, you'll receive:

1. A full-length video edit (including a short trailer intro)

2. 3–5 short-form clips optimized for multiple platforms

3. A copy deck with tailored social post copy

4. Thumbnail and cover art design

Everything's ready-to-publish. No micromanaging required.

Do you upload everything for us?

We deliver all final files—long-form edits, short-form clips, copy decks, thumbnails—ready for upload.

Most teams prefer to handle posting internally, but we're happy to coordinate with your producer directly.

Can you take over posting and social media too?

Yes—we can manage distribution and social publishing at an additional cost.

Just let us know what you need and we'll customize your setup.

What's your turnaround time?

72 hours or less from receiving your footage to delivering polished content.

What platforms do you optimize content for?

YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram, X (Twitter), TikTok, Facebook, and Spotify. If your audience lives there, we'll tailor the content for it.

What do you need from us each week?

Just your raw video file (and any brand assets if we don't already have them). That's it—we'll handle the rest.

What if we already have an in-house editor or social media person?

No problem. We're happy to plug into your existing team and handle the pieces you need help with—editing, clip selection, design, or strategy.

Do you offer a trial or sample edit?

We don't offer free trials.

But we do offer a free intro call to walk through your needs, share our process, and show examples of how we'd help grow your podcast.

Once we've started our engagement, you can pause or cancel anytime.

Is this only for podcasts?

Yes—everything we do is designed for B2B podcasts. That focus is what makes our content perform.

Can we scale beyond the base package?

Absolutely. If you need more clips, additional platforms, or extra deliverables, we'll put together a custom package that fits your goals and workload.
